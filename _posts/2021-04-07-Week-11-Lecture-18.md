##  Nested loops

A **nested loop** is a loop within a loop.  For example

```Matlab
flavors = ["cherry","orange","grape"];
kinds = ["cola", "sucker", "canned fruit"];

for x = flavors;
  for y = kinds;
    display(x + " " + y)
  end
end
```
